Welcome.

Hello there. My name is Matt Heapes, and I am a software and web developer. I have experience programming back to Apple BASIC on a Apple 2e, but more recently I've been learning Java, C#, html5, css, and javaScript. That's all sort of new though...sort of.

I've studied computer programming in one form or another since the fourth grade. I studied it on and off throughout my K-12 years. When I went off to the University of Missouri-Columbia in 1999, I went for a bachelor's in computer science. I ended up graduating with...a B.A. in Technical Theatre!?! I know, how did that happen? That's a story for another time, suffice it to say, it did happen, and I've spent my time since then working all over the country, with many different shows, at many different theaters. From the Santa Fe Opera, to the Alley Theatre in Houston, I ended up settling in Las Vegas. While here in Vegas I've worked for Blue Man Group, Cirque du Soliel and a few others in between. It's been pretty wild.

Well, that brings up to March 2020. I was at work, getting ready for another night in the Automation department at KA by Cirque du Soliel, when you guessed it, 2020 happened. Every show and casino on the strip, shuttered their doors and turned off the lights. We were the amoung the first buisnesses to close, and if there are any shows still financially stable enough to reopen, we will be the last ones to reopen. So we hold together in our tight knit litte communities, each show it's own little suburb of the much larger city of our craft. Each city a part of the hidden world that helps to preserve the art that came before us and create new art all our own. In that preservation and creation we hope to inspire, to educate and to entertain.

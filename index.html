Welcome.

Hello there. My name is Matt Heapes, and I am a software and web developer. I have experience programming back to Apple BASIC on a Apple 2e, but more recently I've been learning Java, html5, CSS, javaScript, a full course about the SDLC, agile development, test driven development and well just check it out for yourself...

I've studied software development in one form or another since the fourth grade. I studied it on and off throughout my K-12 years. When I went off to the University of Missouri-Columbia in 1999, I went for a bachelor's in computer science. I ended up graduating with...a B.A. in Technical Theatre!?! Weird right? it did happen, and I've spent my time since then working all over the country, with many different shows, at many different theaters. From the Santa Fe Opera, to the Alley Theatre in Houston, I ended up settling in Las Vegas. While here in Vegas I've worked for Blue Man Group, Cirque du Soliel and a few others in between. It's been pretty wild.

Well, that brings up to March 2020. I was at work, getting ready for another night at Cirque du Soliel, when you guessed it, 2020 happened. Every show and casino on the strip, shuttered their doors and turned off he lights. We were the amoung the first buisnesses to close. It was hard, but "Life Finds A Way:)" That's how I ended up back in KC at JCCC working thru their Software Developer Certificate prograrm, which I completed this May 15, 2022. I look forward to the next chapter.
